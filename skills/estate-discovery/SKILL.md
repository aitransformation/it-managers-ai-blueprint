# Estate Discovery

Use this when the task is to understand what exists now and where the estate is messy.

## What good looks like

Produce a clear picture of:
- business-critical outcomes
- major business processes
- systems supporting those processes
- data sources/stores
- ownership and support gaps
- simplification opportunities

## Workflow

1. Define the business scope first.
2. Inventory systems, data stores, integrations, identities, devices, and key vendors.
3. Map each major system to a business outcome or process.
4. Flag duplication, unclear ownership, shadow IT, and brittle dependencies.
5. Recommend the smallest useful simplification moves.
