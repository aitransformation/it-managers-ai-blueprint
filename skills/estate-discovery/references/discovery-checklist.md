# Discovery checklist

## Capture
- business functions
- core processes
- application list
- file/data repositories
- identity/auth systems
- endpoints and servers
- network/security controls
- backup locations
- SaaS vendors
- major integrations
- known pain points

## For each major system
- what business outcome does it support?
- who owns it?
- who administers it?
- what data does it hold?
- what breaks if it disappears?
- what other systems depend on it?
- is there duplication or overlap?

## Common findings to flag
- duplicate tools
- nobody owns the system
- unsupported legacy dependency
- unclear data location
- AI use with no policy
- backup exists but restore confidence is weak
